About Me

BSN Graduate

Clinical Nursing Professional & Researcher

I am a dedicated and compassionate nursing professional currently completing my Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) in 4th Year at Fatima Jinnah Medical University (FJMU), Lahore.
